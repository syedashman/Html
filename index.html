Laptop
A laptop is a portable personal computer designed for mobility and convenience. It integrates all the essential components of a desktop computer, including a display, keyboard, and a pointing device, into a single compact unit.
Computer
A computer is an electronic device designed to process data and perform various tasks according to a set of instructions, known as software. Modern computers are versatile and can handle a wide range of applications, from simple calculations to complex simulations.
Mouse
A mouse is a hand-held input device used to interact with a computer. It controls the movement of a cursor on the computer's display screen, allowing users to execute commands and interact with software applications.
Chair
A chair is a piece of furniture designed to provide seating for one person. Chairs are commonly found in homes, offices, and various public spaces, offering comfort and support during a wide range of activities, such as working, dining, and relaxing.
Desk
A desk is a piece of furniture typically used in a workspace or home office, providing a flat surface for writing, reading, and using a computer. Desks are essential for organizing and supporting various work-related activities, enhancing productivity and comfort.
