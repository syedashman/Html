Bed
A bed is a piece of furniture used for sleeping or resting, typically consisting of a frame, mattress, and various bedding accessories. Beds are essential for providing comfort, support, and a good night's sleep, making them a central piece of furniture in any bedroom.
Wheel Chair
A wheelchair is a mobility aid designed to help individuals who have difficulty walking or are unable to walk due to injury, illness, or disability. It consists of a seat mounted on wheels, allowing the user to move independently or with assistance.
BP Apparatus
A blood pressure apparatus, also known as a sphygmomanometer, is a medical device used to measure blood pressure, the force of blood against the walls of the arteries. Accurate blood pressure monitoring is essential for diagnosing and managing conditions like hypertension and other cardiovascular diseases.
Oxygen Mask
An oxygen mask is a medical device used to deliver oxygen from a storage tank to the lungs of a patient. It covers the nose and mouth and ensures that the patient receives an adequate supply of oxygen, which is crucial for treating various respiratory conditions and maintaining overall health.
Medicine
Medicine refers to substances or compounds used to diagnose, treat, and prevent diseases and conditions. It encompasses a broad range of drugs, therapies, and practices designed to promote health and well-being. Medicines are typically prescribed by healthcare professionals and can be administered in various forms.
